Good motion is almost invisible.

The user notices

the change

not

the animation.

Motion is explanation.

Not decoration.

# Premium Test

# Review Questions

Does motion explain change?

Does motion preserve context?

Can one animation be removed?

Is the animation calm?

Would users notice the motion

or only the result?

Does the animation reduce cognitive effort?

# Final Principle

The highest quality animation

is the one

behaves naturally.
